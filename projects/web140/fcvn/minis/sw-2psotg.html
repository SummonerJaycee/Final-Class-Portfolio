about the show

CURRENTLY: COMING SOON    |   RATED: TV-MA    |   FORMAT: 1 HR

CONFLICT is rife throughout the Galaxy. THE EMPIRE and THE REPUBLIC are at war, struggling for alliances to increase their power and bring them one step closer to winning the battle for the supreme influence over the Galaxy. The price is high and the causalities higher, but neither side is showing signs of backing down any time soon.

On CIRCARPOUS V, a misty jungle planet in the CIRCARPOUS SECTOR, things are even more turbulent. The Empire's resource mining operations have begun to take a toll on the environment and the inhabitants have begun to fight back. Desperate to increase their popularity in on the war-torn planet, the Republic deploys their best chance at winning the natives over in such dire conditions --- TITAN SQUAD, their most elite special forces squadron led by AVALARIA LEAHHAN.

The smuggler NER'ATHYN, whose reputation precedes her, and her crew are caught in the crossfire when their services are requested by Republic forces on the ground...
