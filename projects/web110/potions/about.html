Who Am I ?

That's the question, isn't it? In a profession such as this, secrets are not uncommon (you should know this!). My name will remain a mystery to you, dear customer, but you may call me the Potion Master!

I founded the Salem Apothecary to provide those will less expertise, time and care than I who need potions for any variety of reasons! Past customers have told me that appreciate my policy of never asking what they intend to use the potion for; after all, it isn't any of my business and I like to keep it that way! All I ask in return is you promote my little magic shop to your friends and deny its existance to anyone who might not understand us!

Most prices are negotiable and I'm always up for a good trade if you have something better to offer than money! However, all sales are final and no refunds will be given! I can't take back a used potion, now can I? Once the potion has been delivered to you (by my personal couriers, of course!), it can't be returned to me. Why? Well! I don't know what you might've done to it, and any slight alteration could have any number of results, thus making the potion useless to all who desire a specific outcome!

All my listings are pre-made, but I often get asked if I make custom potions on a case-by-case basis. The answer is yes! Contact me directly and we'll discuss what you're looking for!
