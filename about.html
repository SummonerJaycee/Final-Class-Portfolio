About Me

        

Hey ! I'm Jarin & I build things for the web.

I love creating ( and sometimes designing ) fun & beautiful websites, handcoded from scratch.

Diving a little deeper...

I'm 30 years old and anyone who knows me will tell you that I take pride in being a woman in tech. The light of my life is my opinionated 12-year-old chihuahua, Rylie, who was been with me since I turned 18.

My journey into web development began with the desire to build a custom theme for my writing blog on Tumblr. I wanted to stand out from the crowd, to create something unique and tailored just for me. What started as a simple, one-off project became the rocket that would launch me down a path I'd never realized was possible for me.

Cheesy, I know, but it's true !

Since then, I've gone back to school to major in Web Technologies and I've worked with two amazing clients who are doing incredible work for their communities.
